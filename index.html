Since 2016

One oven. One family. No compromise on the char.

Forno Rosso started as a single oak-fired oven that Marco Bellini built by hand in his garage, brick by brick, over one winter. Ten years later, the oven is bigger, the dough still rests exactly 48 hours, and the leopard-spotted char on every crust is still the whole point.

We buy from three farms within forty miles, mill nothing ourselves, and close the kitchen the moment the dough for tomorrow is ready — not a minute before.
